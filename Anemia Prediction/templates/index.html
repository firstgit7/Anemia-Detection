Anemia Detector
Quick health check

Estimate anemia risk in under a minute.

Provide your recent blood test values and this model will estimate the likelihood of anemia. The tool is easy to use, mobile‑friendly, and visually guided.

💊
How it works

The model uses your blood indices to classify whether the pattern looks similar to anemia cases.

It is an educational tool and cannot replace a laboratory or doctor.

Parameters used
Hemoglobin Oxygen‑carrying protein.
MCV Cell size.
MCH/MCHC Hemoglobin amount & concentration.
Gender Adjusts normal ranges.
This demo is for learning purposes only and should not guide medical decisions.
